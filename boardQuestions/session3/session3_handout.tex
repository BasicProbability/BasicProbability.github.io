\section{Memorylessness}
Assume that $ X \sim Geometric(p) $. Show that the geometric distribution is memoryless (or stationary), i.e.
show that 
$$ P(X = n + k | X \geq n) = P(X = k) $$
where $ n,k > 0 $.
